FEVER:
Symptoms: high temperature above 38C, chills, sweating, headache, muscle aches
Severity: Low if below 39C, High if above 40C
Treatment: rest, fluids, paracetamol

CHEST PAIN:
Symptoms: pressure in chest, shortness of breath, pain in left arm
Severity: Critical - possible heart attack
Treatment: immediate emergency care

DIABETES:
Symptoms: frequent urination, excessive thirst, blurred vision, fatigue
Severity: Medium to High
Treatment: insulin, diet control, medication

ASTHMA:
Symptoms: wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, coughing
Severity: Medium to Critical
Treatment: inhaler, avoid triggers, bronchodilators

MIGRAINE:
Symptoms: severe headache, nausea, sensitivity to light and sound
Severity: Low to Medium
Treatment: rest in dark room, pain medication, hydration

PNEUMONIA:
Symptoms: cough with phlegm, fever, chills, difficulty breathing
Severity: High
Treatment: antibiotics, rest, hospitalization if severe
